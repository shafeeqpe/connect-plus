About Us

Connect Plus Pvt. Ltd., a global manpower service provider, has a legacy of more than two decades in providing end to end solutions in recruitment and ensuring the right people are brought in for the right jobs.

Connect plus has a professional team who partners with our esteemed customers in providing customized recruitment and staffing solutions with a global foot print in diversified industries and business portfolios.

The company provides a range of services from recruitment of blue collared and white collared employees for temporary, permanent and contract requirements to employee assessment & selection, training, outsourcing and consulting etc.

Headquartered in Kochi, Kerala Connect Plus is equipped with excellent facilities and infrastructure to facilitate our clients. We have a range of professional associates across India who support us in our recruitment service. Being an integral part of Manappat Group we leverage our presence and brand equity in the UAE, Oman & the United Kingdom Our operations have been approved by Ministry of External Affairs, Govt. of India vide its license No. B0837/KER/COM/1000+/5/5011/97. Since establishment, we have enjoyed an impressive record of consistent growth and superior performance and we are fully geared up to meet any human resource requirements of our clients.
